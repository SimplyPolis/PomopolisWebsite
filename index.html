About

Pomopolis Farms is a family owned and operated Dorper sheep ranch. We specialize in selling sheep directly to you for whatever your needs may be. We raise our sheep free-range with a grass-fed diet.

Many customers decide to butcher our sheep due to the delicious meat and capability to conform to religious doctrine. However, our sheep can be used in other capacities to graze land to keep the grasses down or to breed.

All sheep are priced based on weight, and availability is dependent on the season. If you are ordering for a holiday, you should contact us early to ensure we can reserve you a sheep.

Herd

The photos above show our herd of Dorper sheep out grazing. Dorper sheep do not grow wool to the same extent as other breeds of sheep, making them suitable for more arid climates. Its meat has a unique lean taste.

LOCATION

Our farm is located in Wheatland, a farming town in California's Central Valley. This location allows the easy growing of grasses to feed our herd. Further information on the sheep and for purchase inquiries, please use the contact info below.
